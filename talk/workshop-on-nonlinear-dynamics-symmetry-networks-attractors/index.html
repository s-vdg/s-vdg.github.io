Workshop on Nonlinear Dynamics – Symmetry, Networks, Attractors

Conference

Abstract

The workshop will highlight, recount, and discuss historic and, most prominently, recent developments in the mathematical research subject of nonlinear dynamics and bifurcations with a particular focus on methods exploiting underlying structures.

The Workshop on Nonlinear Dynamics will be held at the University of Hamburg from July 24 until July 26, 2024.

On this occasion, we will also anticipate Reiner Lauterbach’s 70th birthday later that year and celebrate his contributions to the field of nonlinear dynamics.
